## 5.4 Building Generational Wealth

### The Three Generations Rule

> "Shirtsleeves to shirtsleeves in three generations" - Common proverb

**Generation 1:** Creates wealth (hard work, sacrifice)
**Generation 2:** Maintains wealth (education, discipline)
**Generation 3:** Destroys wealth (entitlement, no skills)

### How to Break the Cycle

#### 1. Financial Education for Children
Start teaching at age:
- **5-10:** Value of money, saving for goals
- **10-15:** Budgeting, compound interest, investing basics
- **15-20:** Stocks, mutual funds, real-world investing
- **20+:** Full financial responsibility

## 5.5 The Final Framework: 10 Rules for Lifelong Wealth

### Rule 1: Pay Yourself First
```
Income â†’ Savings (20%+) â†’ Expenses
NOT
Income â†’ Expenses â†’ Savings (whatever's left)
```

### Rule 2: Live Below Your Means
- Your lifestyle should lag your income
- Lifestyle inflation destroys wealth
- The millionaire next door drives a Toyota

### Rule 3: Invest Early, Invest Often
```
â‚¹5,000/month from age 25 @ 12% = â‚¹3.2 Crores at 60
â‚¹10,000/month from age 35 @ 12% = â‚¹1.9 Crores at 60

Starting early > Investing more later
```

### Rule 4: Understand What You Own
- Never invest in what you don't understand
- If you can't explain it, don't buy it
- Complexity is not sophistication

### Rule 5: Keep Costs Low
```
Impact of expense ratio over 30 years on â‚¹10,000/month SIP:

0.1% expense (Index): â‚¹3.54 Crores
1.5% expense (Active): â‚¹2.83 Crores
```

### Rule 6: Diversify Wisely
- Across asset classes (equity, debt, gold)
- Across geographies (India, US, emerging)
- Across time (SIP, not lumpsum)
- But not over-diversify (max 7-10 funds)

### Rule 7: Stay the Course
- Markets will crash (2008, 2020, etc.)
- Don't sell in panic
- Crashes are opportunities
- Time in market > Timing the market

### Rule 8: Rebalance Regularly
- Annual rebalancing at minimum
- Sell high, buy low mechanically
- Remove emotions from investing

### Rule 9: Protection Before Growth
- Adequate term insurance
- Health insurance
- Emergency fund
- Then invest for growth

### Rule 10: Keep Learning
- Read investment books
- Follow quality sources
- Learn from mistakes
- Adapt as needed
