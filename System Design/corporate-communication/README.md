## Key Principles

### 1. **Clarity Over Cleverness**
- Be direct and specific
- Avoid jargon when communicating with non-technical stakeholders
- Use examples to illustrate complex points

### 2. **Audience Awareness**
- Adjust technical depth based on your audience
- Consider their priorities and concerns
- Use appropriate formality level

### 3. **Timeliness**
- Respond promptly to urgent requests
- Set expectations if you need time to investigate
- Don't let messages languish

### 4. **Documentation**
- Write things down for future reference
- Keep important decisions in searchable formats
- Update documentation when things change

### 5. **Emotional Intelligence**
- Read the room (or the chat)
- Be empathetic and considerate
- Manage your own reactions professionally

## Communication Channels

| Channel | Best For | Response Time | Formality |
|---------|----------|---------------|-----------|
| Email | Non-urgent, detailed info, documentation | 24 hours | Formal |
| Slack/Teams | Quick questions, updates, collaboration | 1-4 hours | Semi-formal |
| Video Call | Complex discussions, sensitive topics | Scheduled | Formal |
| In-Person | Relationship building, brainstorming | Immediate | Varies |
| Phone | Urgent matters, quick clarifications | Immediate | Semi-formal |

## Common Communication Mistakes to Avoid

1. ❌ Being too vague about blockers or timelines
2. ❌ Overusing "just checking in" without adding value
3. ❌ Sending walls of text without structure
4. ❌ Not reading the full thread before replying
5. ❌ Using only technical jargon with business stakeholders
6. ❌ Waiting until the deadline to communicate delays
7. ❌ Not confirming understanding in critical conversations
8. ❌ Being defensive when receiving feedback

## Quick Reference Templates

### Status Update Template
```
**What I completed:** [specific accomplishments]
**What I'm working on:** [current tasks]
**Blockers:** [issues needing resolution]
**ETA:** [realistic timeline]
```

### Asking for Help Template
```
**Context:** [what you're trying to achieve]
**What I've tried:** [steps already taken]
**Specific question:** [clear, focused ask]
**Urgency:** [timeline/priority]
```

### Disagreement Template
```
I see your point about [acknowledge their view].
My concern is [state your perspective with data/reasoning].
Could we consider [propose alternative or compromise]?
```
